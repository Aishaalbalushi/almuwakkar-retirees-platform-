ABOUT AL MUWAKKAR

A digital platform created for life after retirement.

Al Muwakkar is a senior project built to improve retirees' quality of life through easier access to useful services, social activities, financial benefits, and wellbeing support.

Empower retirees to stay active

To become the leading platform that empowers retirees to stay active, engaged, and valued in their community.

OUR SERVICES

Explore Al Muwakkar Services

Part Time Jobs

Retirees can browse and apply for suitable job opportunities posted by companies.

Travel Programs

Users can explore and register for different types of trips such as cultural, relaxation, and adventure trips.

Clubs

Retirees can join interest-based WhatsApp groups such as reading, gardening, art, and social clubs.

Events & Volunteering

The platform provides community events and volunteer opportunities to encourage social participation.

Discount Card System

Retirees can apply for the Al Muwakkar discount card to receive discounts from participating stores and businesses.

Health Support Service

Includes mental support through chatting with the Alwaqar bot, plus meeting a trainer who guides retirees in physical fitness.
